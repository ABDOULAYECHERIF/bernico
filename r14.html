About the Bernico RXP14

The Bernico RXP14 is the flagship of the Bernico range – a 14-meter masterpiece that blends sheer power with ultimate comfort.

Its striking design, unmatched stability, and exhilarating speed make it the perfect choice for those who demand more from the water.

Spacious enough for family and friends, yet agile and responsive at any pace, the RXP14 transforms every journey into a first-class experience. Born from Bernico’s racing DNA, it redefines what a performance powerboat can be.

Specifications

Width 3.48 m
Draft 0.75
Weight Hull 2800 kg
Fuel Capacity 800 L
Fresh Water Cap 150 L

Engine Application Single, double, Triple or Quad
Materials All composite
