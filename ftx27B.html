About the Bernico FTX -B

The Bernico FTX 27 B is built for those who crave performance and precision.

At 27 feet, this powerboat combines an aggressive, streamlined design with Bernico’s proven racing hull technology, delivering breathtaking speed and razor-sharp handling.

Comfortable seating and smart deck layout make it just as enjoyable for relaxed cruising with friends as for adrenaline-fueled runs at full throttle. The FTX 27 B embodies Bernico’s promise: performance without compromise.
